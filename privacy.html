Privacy Policy

 

This Privacy Policy describes the handling of user data by the Tap.Dot 2 and explains what information Futch Production Syndicate (FPS) collects about you and why. Your privacy and personal data are always respected, protected and handled with care.

 

Data We Receive/Store:

  • Your Google Play username to associate you with any levels you create with the app,
  • User text and level formatting data included in each level you create

 

Data We Transfer:

  • Acheivement data may be sent to your Application Store (i.e. Google Play or Apple Store) to inform you which Acheivements you have completed /li>
  • Application usage data may be transferred to Google Play Analytics /li>

 

Note that pictures taken with the in-app QR scanner are not stored or transferred and are processed soley in-app.

 

Data Transferring and Sharing:

Data sent to FPS is transferred securely. FPS protects your information in accordance with the law. FPS does not store any personally identifying information.

 

Processed data:

To Google Analytics:

  • ID used for granting the Software access to the computer's camera,
  • ID used for opening levels in the game,
  • Information about the use of the product's user interface, including information about the opening of the interface's windows (including identifiers and names of windows and used control elements) and switching between windows, type of the user's interaction with the interface, information about changes to settings and product parameters (including the name of the setting or parameter, and the new values).

Data Transferring and Sharing:

Data is forwarded to Google Analytics over a secure channel, access to information and its protection are governed by the relevant terms of service of Apps Flyer, Google Analytics.
